After migrating from Google Chrome to Firefox, I investigated all the privacy related extensions and options. [Multi-Account Containers's extension](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/containers) is awesome and I ended up using it for shopping and basic VPN proxy for specific websites. For Google sites, I was inspired by [philsnow's gist](https://gist.github.com/philsnow/322bea2867d401171ada1c2eb7a38c89), that helps setup a Google-based container, similar to how the Facebook container works.

[View gist on GitHub](https://gist.github.com/cwparsons/6cc97794944e4aa3f09612c8c9e9381b).
